# ZenBreak Gamification Design

**Goal:** Make break-taking addictive through streaks, leveling, and daily challenges.

**Where it shows:** Menu dropdown (status) + break overlay (rewards on completion).

## XP System

| Action | XP |
|--------|-----|
| Complete a break | +10 |
| Complete with video demo | +15 |
| First break of the day | +20 bonus |
| Complete daily challenge | +50 |

## Health Ranks

| Level | Title | XP Required |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Beginner | 0 |
| 2 | Aware | 100 |
| 3 | Active | 300 |
| 4 | Consistent | 600 |
| 5 | Dedicated | 1000 |
| 6 | Athlete | 1500 |
| 7 | Guardian | 2500 |
| 8 | Zen Master | 4000 |

## Streaks

- Minimum 3 breaks/day to maintain streak
- Streak freeze: miss one day, streak survives once
- Shown in menu: "12d streak"

## Daily Challenges (one per day, rotating)

- Complete 5 breaks today
- Hit all 6 body areas
- Take 3 wrist breaks
- Complete a break before 10am
- No skips — do every break offered
- Watch 2 demo videos

## Menu Display

```
Next break in 12 min
5 breaks today · 12d streak
Level 4: Consistent · 620 XP
Challenge: Hit all 6 body areas (4/6)
```

## Break Overlay

After clicking "I did it", title briefly shows "Nice! +10 XP" before dismissing.
